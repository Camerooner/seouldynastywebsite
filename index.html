There's an esports league for my favorite video game, Overwatch, called the Overwatch League. My favorite team that competes in the league is called the Seoul Dynasty, and I wanted to make a website dedicated to the history of the players they've had on their team throughout the years of the leagues existance, since 2018 to be specific. Overwatch is a 5v5 team oriented first person shooter, where the goal is to capture different objectives across various different maps and locations. You can choose and play with up to 36 different heroes as of March of 2023, separated by three different roles: Tank, Damage and Support. In this website, I'll have a home page for the Seoul Dynasty team, as well as three differnt webpages for each role in Overwatch. Each player that has been picked up by the Seoul Dynasty specializes in one of these three roles, so I'll be able to organize it through which role they play, what year they were picked up by the Dynasty, and which Overwatch heros they specialized in. I'm super passionate about this and have always wanted to make a website for this team, so I'm excited to make this!
